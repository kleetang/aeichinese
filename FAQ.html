FAQ:

If I miss the one lesson, can I make it up?

Yes, you can always make up your missed lesson by joining in our other classes. But please email us as early as possible, so we can let you switch to the other class for free of charge. We just don't want you to miss any lesson. You can pick any schedule you want at your most convenient time for our lessons.

What can I do if, for some reason, I have to quit during the lessons?

We’ll give you our school credit and you can keep it for 1 year. By whatever reason, if you decide to quit at any time, we will give you the full balance credit back. For example, you paid for 22 lessons, and you decide to quit after the 4th lesson, we will give you the balance of 18 lessons credit back. You can always come back within a year for continuing your 5th lesson at the next term. We won’t charge you again.
