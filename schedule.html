Schedule a Visit

To get a true feel for AEI-Learn Chinese and our gold standard service in Chinese language classes, why not schedule a time to come and visit us? We would love nothing more than to guide you around the school, show you our state of the art facilities, introduce you to our teachers, and let you hear from our current students about their AEI-Learn Chinese experience face to face.

You may even be lucky enough to see one of our classes in action as our students have Chinese conversations with each other or play group games with their peers. A visit to the Academy will allow you to get a feel for our learning process, and offers the opportunity to ask any burning questions that you might have about your learning needs and how we can help you achieve your goals.

Contact us today and schedule your visit – we can’t wait to meet you.
