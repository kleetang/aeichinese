Welcome to AEI-Intlingo Academy

where learning the Chinese language is both fun and produces unparalleled results. At the heart of our ethos is the student experience, and we tailor each of our classes to meet the individual needs of every student to ensure that they learn and retain both written and spoken Chinese language skills. From Children’s weekend classes through to Business Chinese and Corporate training, our classes offer many styles of learning, always commandeered by the most experienced and highly qualified Chinese language teachers working in the industry today.

We 100% believe in the learning capacities of each of our students, and it is our daily pleasure to be able to realize every AEI-Intlingo Academy student’s potential to the fullest.
