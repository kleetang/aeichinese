Business Group Classes

The increasingly important role that China plays in global business means that acquisition of the Chinese language is in greater demand than ever before. At the AEI-Learn Chinese we teach Business Chinese at all levels so that beginners can form a grasp of basic business terms and uses of language, while those students already conducting business with Chinese partners and clients will be given the tools to significantly enhance their communications.

Beginning Level 1

This level is quite suitable for adults who have no prior knowledge of the Chinese language and are interested in learning Mandarin Chinese.

Beginning Level 2

Level 2 classes are scheduled for students who finished Level 1 from our program, or students who passed our level 1 test could attend Level 2 classes as well.
